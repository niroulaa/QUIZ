RULES OF THE QUIZ

  • When the player chooses an option , he/she cannot choose other options. Hence, your first answer will be your final answer.
  • The player cannot go back to the previous question after he/she clicks the "next" button on the bottom right corner. Hence,the user can't skip a question.
  • Whenever the player refreshes the browser , a new set of 25 questions will be provided and all your progress with previous questions will be lost.
